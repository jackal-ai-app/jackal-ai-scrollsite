01 —

Overview and Scope

Jackal AI ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information when you use our website, products, and services.

Protecting your personal information is a priority for Jackal AI. This Privacy Policy applies to Jackal AI and governs how we collect, use, and handle personal information through our website, applications, and services. Jackal AI provides AI-powered voice systems, automation, and related business services.

By using our website or services, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy.

02 —

Our Role: Controller and Processor

For our website, direct enquiries, marketing activities, and self-serve sign-ups, Jackal AI acts as a data controller for the purposes of Australian privacy law.

Where Jackal AI provides services to business customers that involve processing personal information on their behalf (including call handling, transcription, and automation services), Jackal AI acts as a data processor and processes information strictly in accordance with customer instructions and applicable agreements.

Where we act as a processor, any applicable data processing agreement will prevail in the event of inconsistency with this Privacy Policy.

03 —

Collection of Personal Information

3.1 Personal Information

To provide our services, we may collect personal information that can be used to identify or contact you, including but not limited to:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business details
  • Address, city, state, and postcode
  • Account and billing information

We collect personal information that you provide directly (for example, when you contact us, sign up for services, or make a purchase), as well as information collected automatically.

We may use this information to communicate with you about our services, deliver requested services, provide updates, and send marketing communications. You may opt out of marketing communications at any time using the unsubscribe mechanism provided.

3.1.1 — AI Voice and Communications Data

If you enable AI voice features or connect telephony services, we may receive call audio, call recordings, transcripts, call metadata, and interaction logs ("Communications Data").

Communications Data is used to deliver services, maintain system functionality, improve reliability and safety, and provide customer support. Business customers are responsible for ensuring appropriate notices and consents are obtained from callers before enabling call recording or processing.

We may use Communications Data in aggregated or de-identified form to improve and enhance our services, provided it does not identify individual users.

3.1.2 — Sensitive Information

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information unless it is reasonably necessary for service delivery and you have expressly provided it, and where required by law, given consent.

3.2 Usage Data

We may collect information about how our website and services are accessed and used ("Usage Data"). This may include IP address, browser type and version, pages visited, time and date of visits, time spent on pages, device identifiers, and diagnostic data.

When accessing our services via a mobile device, we may also collect device type, operating system, mobile browser type, and similar technical information.

3.3 Location Data

Where permitted by your device settings, we may collect approximate location information to support service functionality and improve user experience. You can enable or disable location services at any time through your device settings.

3.4 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate and improve our website and services. Cookies may be session-based or persistent and help us remember preferences, improve security, analyse usage, and deliver relevant content.

You can configure your browser to refuse cookies or notify you when cookies are used. Some parts of our website may not function correctly if cookies are disabled.

04 —

Use of Personal Information

We collect and use personal information for purposes including:

  • Providing and maintaining our services
  • Managing accounts, subscriptions, and billing
  • Communicating service updates and operational notices
  • Providing customer support
  • Analysing and improving service performance
  • Monitoring usage and detecting technical issues
  • Enforcing contractual rights and obligations
  • Sending marketing communications where permitted
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations

We only use personal information where it is reasonably necessary for our business operations or as otherwise permitted by law.

05 —

Retention of Information

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Usage Data is generally retained for shorter periods unless required for security, functionality improvements, or legal compliance. Where practicable, we anonymise or aggregate information instead of retaining identifiable data.

06 —

International and Overseas Data Processing

Personal information may be stored or processed on servers located outside Australia, including by third-party Service Providers.

Where personal information is transferred or processed overseas, we take reasonable steps to ensure that recipients handle personal information in a manner consistent with Australian privacy requirements. However, we are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party providers once information is handled by their systems.

We may engage third-party companies and individuals ("Service Providers") to facilitate our services, provide services on our behalf, perform service-related tasks, or assist us in analysing how our services are used.

These Service Providers may have access to personal information only to the extent required to perform these functions on our behalf. They are contractually obligated to handle personal information in a manner consistent with applicable privacy laws and not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

08 —

Sharing Information with Third Parties

Jackal AI does not sell, rent, or lease customer lists or personal information to third parties.

We may share personal information with trusted third-party partners where necessary to:

  • Perform statistical analysis
  • Provide customer support
  • Deliver services
  • Operate and maintain our systems
  • Communicate with users

All such third parties are required to maintain the confidentiality of personal information and are prohibited from using it for any purpose other than providing services to Jackal AI.

We may disclose personal information without notice where required to do so by law, or where we reasonably believe disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations or lawful requests
  • Protect and defend the rights or property of Jackal AI
  • Prevent or investigate unlawful activity
  • Protect the personal safety of users or the public
09 —

Call Recording

Phone calls may be recorded for quality assurance, training, automation, analytics, and service delivery purposes.

By continuing a call, you consent to call recording and processing as described in this Privacy Policy.

10 —

Right to Deletion

You may request the deletion of your personal information by contacting us with a verifiable request.

Subject to applicable legal exceptions, we will:

  • Delete personal information from our records; and
  • Take reasonable steps to direct our Service Providers to delete personal information from their records where applicable.

We may be unable to comply with deletion requests where the information is required to:

  • Fulfil contractual obligations or provide requested services
  • Detect, prevent, or investigate security incidents or unlawful activity
  • Debug or maintain system functionality
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Maintain records required under Australian law
  • Use information internally in a lawful and reasonable manner consistent with your relationship with us
11 —

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices, services, or legal requirements.

Where changes are significant, we will take reasonable steps to notify you, including by email, website notice, or service notification.

Your continued use of our services after changes take effect constitutes acknowledgment of the updated Privacy Policy. Where required by law, we will obtain your consent to material changes.
